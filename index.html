Why Choose Eco Lanka?

Ethics

Here at Eco Lanka, taking care of the environment and our clients are our top priority. All our partner companies ensure that their production processes are taken care of. Waves Lanka sources all of it’s rubber for their flip flops locally. Every single product purchased through us supports various Sri Lankan farming communities, allowing them to achieve sustainable and safe livelihoods.

Environment

Our company is based around improving the environment. That’s why we have partnered up with Sri Lanka’s top eco friendly companies such as; Waves, Eco Maximus and Eco Kadé. We’re trying to turn around the pollution caused by the usage of plastic by substituting it for entirely organic alternatives. All our products are biodegradable and consists of varying substances such as; natural rubber, bamboo, elephant dung etc.
