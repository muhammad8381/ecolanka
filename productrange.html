Our Product Range

Here at Eco Lanka, along with our partner companies we have some of the finest organic, eco-friendly products that Sri Lanka has to offer. Our products are of the highest quality made from 100% locally sourced raw materials such as rubber, bamboo etc and high standards of quality are maintined to ensure our customers recieve products of the highest grade. Below are a few of the eco friendly products from our partner companies:

Waves: Flip Flops



Our handmade 100% eco-friendly, ethically sourced natural Sri Lankan rubber flip flops will not only mold your foot shape to provide exceptional comfort and support, but also protect our environment for future generations as all of our flip flops are 100% biodegradable and recyclable.

Eco Kadé: Bamboo Toothbrush



The easiest stepping stone to a zero waste toilet is our bamboo toothbrushes. These eco-friendly bamboo toothbrushes are made of 100% bamboo, and nylon is used to make the bristles. On this toothbrush, the medium soft bristles will make sure your teeth are brushed effectively while protecting your gums.

Eco Maximus: Notebooks



The notebooks are handmade and come in a variety of colours, made from 75% elephant dung and 25% recycled paper. There are approximately 60 different color pages inside each one, which are easy to write on with the pen of your choice. It uses only natural dyes and the paper is acid-free.
